About me

I’m a 20-year-old student from Belgrade, currently in my third year at ITS – Comtrade. My passion for 3D art started back in high school, which motivated me to choose a college that would help me learn and grow in this field.

I’ve been a gamer since I was a kid, always fascinated by the worlds and characters in video games. That’s why I now want to learn how to create characters — to bring them to life visually and, hopefully, be part of a game development team someday. My dream is that future gamers will see my work and say, “Wow, this looks amazing.”

Besides gaming, I love photography, traveling, and cats. I often stream on Twitch, sharing my gaming moments. I’m also passionate about video filming and editing — during high school, I even won first place in a video editing competition.
I’m always curious, eager to learn new skills, and excited about creative challenges.
